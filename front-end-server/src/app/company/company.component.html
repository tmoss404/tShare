tShare

This web application was made so teachers could share documentation in a quick, easy-to-use, and reliable way, allowing us to make their jobs a little easier and letting them get back to teach faster.

Features

This app features the ability to upload files directly onto the system from your local machine and share them ith your colleagues, it also allows you to directly share files with others while managing the level of access such as read and write.
